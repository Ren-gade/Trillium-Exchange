The Rules

Each participant will be assigned a recipient for whom they will craft something in the SCA art form of their choice. Each participant will receive their recipient's information shortly after sign up.

Each participant will also be assigned to another participant as that person's recipient.

Materials cost for the item created should not exceed $25 (use of "stash" materials is exempt).

Delivery of the item (not just crafting) must be completed on or before the Handoff date.

Check-ins

There will be three check-in deadlines. Participants must confirm progress with the secretary. Failure to communicate may result in suspension or banning from future exchanges.

If your contact info changes, you are responsible for updating the admin team.

Withdrawals

The deadline to withdraw without penalty is the second check-in date. After that, completion is expected unless a serious emergency occurs.

Extensions & Completion

Extensions of up to 4 weeks may be granted at admin discretion. Failure to deliver will result in being banned from future exchanges.

The goal: everyone who makes a gift receives one in return.

Participants must agree to all rules, meet deadlines, keep contact info updated, and communicate issues early.
