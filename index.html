About Trillium Exchange

The Trillium Exchange is a gift exchange within Ealdormere that celebrates generosity, creativity, and the skills of its artisans. Participants are paired with another artisan and create a handcrafted gift tailored to their recipient's interests, hobbies, and persona.

First step is that you will fill out a survey regarding your likes, dislikes, sizes, allergies, what kind of things you do, and so on. Each participant will be matched with another artisan for whom they will create something personal. Once you receive the name of your recipient then you will have a set time to create a beautiful gift for one lucky person, spending no more than $25 for materials (plus anything already in your stash). Remember to keep your admin team updated on your progress, and if you need help or information we are here to help.
