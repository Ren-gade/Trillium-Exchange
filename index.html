Welcome to the Trillium Exchange

This year we are leaving you free to choose the gift you feel is most appropriate to your recipient's persona, and your own skills and interests as an artisan.

The Trillium Exchange lets our immensely talented artisans in Ealdormere get to know each other a little better by working on a special project for a single recipient.

About Trillium Exchange

The Trillium Exchange is a gift exchange within Ealdormere that celebrates generosity, creativity, and the skills of its artisans.

Participants are paired with another artisan and create a handcrafted gift tailored to their recipient's interests, hobbies, and persona.
